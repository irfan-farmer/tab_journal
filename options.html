TabJournal Settings

What TabJournal stores: a timestamped list of your open tabs' titles and addresses, saved only on this device using the browser's local storage. Nothing is ever uploaded — there are no servers, accounts, analytics, or network requests of any kind. You can export or permanently delete everything below.

Capture

Master switch. When off, nothing is recorded at all.
Also snapshot on tab create/remove/update (debounced), not just on the timer.
Bursts of tab events within this window collapse into one snapshot.

Privacy

Off (recommended): store only the site + path and drop query strings and fragments, which often contain tokens or personal data. On: keep the complete URL.
Subdomains are included automatically (excluding example.com also excludes mail.example.com).

Data

Oldest snapshots are trimmed once this cap is reached.
Permanently removes every stored snapshot from this device.
